Design Principles - Bryce Blignaut


Color

Luno Crypto
www.luno.com

Luno uses a monochromatic color scheme in their website. In this specific example it has been used very well. The lighter color of blue is used to emphasize the sign up button, making it easier for users to see where they need to sign up. The colors contrast well and are smooth.

Balance and Alignment

Dodge Automobiles
www.dodge.com


Balance is aided by space. The space between each car helps the site visitor see the difference between cars in a non-overwhelimg way. The car model pictures are evenly spaced and aides in easily finding information. The price and model name are balanced and used in the same way with plenty of space.

Repetition

Rand Merchant Bank
www.rmb.co.za

Rand Merchant Bank uses regular repetition for their menu options. Each option uses a sketch and uses the same colors. This shows emphasis on the learn more icons. The pattern is appealing and they use the same colors in other parts of their menu.
