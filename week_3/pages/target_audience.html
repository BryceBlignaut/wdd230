Target Audience

The target audinece for this site will be local business owners and potential investors.
The Chamber of commerce will provide opportunities for both of these audiences to grow their businesses.

Two Personas

Travis - The Local Business Owner

Visits Jackson Commerce to find information on local businesses and to get marketing for his own venture in town.

Enjoys being involved with the community and wants to consider becoming a member of something bigger than himself.

Needs information on the history of the town.

User Motivation

Motivated by the need to gain business and becoming involved with the community.

Looking for information on town events and business meetups.

Mike - The Homey Investor

Mike is from out of town but has an interest in Jackson Hole.

He wants to expand his business and see if he can start a business or invest in an existing business.

He is considering retirement, but wants to know if Jackson Hole has the necessary amenities he would want close by.

He is also involved with his community and wants do the same thin in retirement. He values community and friendship.

User Motivation

Looking for investment opportunities.

Wants to find town amenities.

Wants community involvement in events.
